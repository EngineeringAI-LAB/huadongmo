Focus Resilience, AI, energy, and complex engineered systems

Huadong Mo is an Associate Professor at UNSW Canberra and the Inaugural Director of the RISER Hub Physics Lab. His research builds intelligent methods for systems that must keep working under uncertainty, extreme events, and changing operational conditions.

His work connects machine learning, reliability engineering, cyber-physical systems, clean energy, and lifelong asset management. Through Engineering-AI Lab, it also extends toward AI engineering, 3D world models, and intelligent agents with a practical eye toward deployable engineering intelligence.
